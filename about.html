About

As an Animation Director with over a decade of experience, I have had the pleasure of directing and animating on more than 10 shipped titles, including the likes of Hogwarts Legacy, For Honor, Disney Infinity, and Fable.

My expertise lies in creating fluid and responsive gameplay animations and systems, collaborating closely with programmers and designers to bring ideas to life. I also take pride in mentoring animators of all levels, sharing my knowledge and experience to help them grow in their craft.

With my strong planning skills, I ensure that projects are delivered on time and to a high standard. I am always eager to expand my knowledge and abilities, and am excited to take on new and challenging projects.
