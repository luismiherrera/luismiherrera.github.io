For Honor

I had the opportunity to work on a great variety of animation tasks in this game, from directing cinematics and the creation and implementation of in-game hero animations, to cosmetic and environmental animations on different maps.

Big thanks to the super-talented team at Ubisoft's Montreal for allowing us to be part of the development of such an amazing game!
